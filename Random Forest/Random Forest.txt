## Out of Bag (00B) :-
It's a built-in validation method in bagging that lets us estimate model performance without a
separate validation set.

OOB Sample = As some data points are being selected repeatedly, Those datasets which are not being
             chosen during the data sampling process.
OOB Accuracy = The accuracy we get after using the OOB samples as our validation data.
OOB Error = The error we get after using the OOB samples as our validation data.
