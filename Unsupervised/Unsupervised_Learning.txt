#Unsupervised learning :- It is a type of machine learning that analyzes unlabeled datasets to identify 
hidden patterns, structures, or relationships without human guidance. 


@Clustering problem: 
It is an unsupervised ML technique that automatically groups similar, unlabeled data points into
clusters based on defined similarity metrics.
1. Partioning Based Clustering Algorithm - KMeans
2. Hierarchial Clustering
3. Density Based Clustering - DBSCAN


KMeans Clustering:- 
-> Choose/Set K (where K is the number of clusters)
-> Randomly initialize K centroids (A centroid is present at the mean position of all points in a cluster)
-> For all the points, minimum distance from the point and all centroids is calculated,
   i.e, min(dist(pt,all centroid))
   To calculate this distance, formulas can be used:
                              a. Euclidean Formula
                              b. Manhattan Formula
-> The centroids are assigned to the closest distance point
-> Above two Steps are repeated
-> New centroid point will be at the mean position of all points in a cluster
